Projects

Gunslinger

Gunslinger is an adaptation of the board game Bang! into a video game. The game is multiplayer, and to fill empty player positions, we are implementing reinforcement learning techniques. We are currently developing it as our graduation project with two other friends.

Engine and Tools: Unity

Do Not Try At Home

Do Not Try at Home is a 3D game where players take on the role of professional stunt drivers, engaging in destruction and combats in movie-like environments. Currently, my friend and I are developing it as part of our coursework.

Engine and Tools: Unity

Deep Blue Rescue

Deep Blue Rescue is a game where players take on the role of underwater guardians tasked with cleaning debris and rescuing marine life. A helper robot assists players by clearing assigned debris, while pollution levels rise against the clock. We developed it for a coursework project with two other friends over the course of one term.

Engine and Tools: Babylon.js, Webgl2
